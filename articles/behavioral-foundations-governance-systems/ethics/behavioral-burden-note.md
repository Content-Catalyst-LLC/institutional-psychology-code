# Behavioral Burden Note

Governance systems distribute behavioral burdens.

A burden analysis should ask:

- Who must learn the most rules?
- Who must provide the most documentation?
- Who experiences the greatest psychological cost?
- Who faces the highest appeal cost?
- Who receives procedural flexibility?
- Who is monitored most closely?
- Who is blamed when governance fails?
- Who has voice in design and revision?
- Can people contest decisions safely?

A governance system can be formally neutral and behaviorally unequal.
