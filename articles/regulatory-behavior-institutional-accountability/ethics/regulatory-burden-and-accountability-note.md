# Regulatory Burden and Accountability Note

Regulatory systems distribute burden and accountability.

A burden and accountability analysis should ask:

- Who must understand the rule?
- Who must document compliance?
- Who pays the cost of compliance?
- Who is monitored most closely?
- Who can negotiate flexibility?
- Who can absorb penalties?
- Who can appeal decisions?
- Who is protected when regulation works?
- Who is exposed when regulation fails?
- Does accountability reach powerful actors or only visible actors?

A regulatory system can be formally neutral and substantively unequal.
