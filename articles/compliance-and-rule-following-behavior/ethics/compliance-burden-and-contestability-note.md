# Compliance Burden and Contestability Note

Compliance systems distribute work, proof, risk, and consequence.

A compliance burden analysis should ask:

- Who must understand the rule?
- Who must prove compliance?
- Who pays the cost of documentation?
- Who is monitored most closely?
- Who can ask questions without risk?
- Who can correct mistakes?
- Who can appeal decisions?
- Who is protected when compliance works?
- Who is harmed when compliance is rigid?
- Does the system distinguish inability from willful violation?

A compliance system can be formally neutral and substantively unequal.
