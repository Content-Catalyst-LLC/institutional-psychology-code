# Coordination Validation Checklist

Before adapting this scaffold to a real case, review:

- [ ] Is the coordination problem clearly defined?
- [ ] Are the actors and required alignment specified?
- [ ] Are possible equilibria or standards documented?
- [ ] Is the focal point identified?
- [ ] Is authority signal strength assessed?
- [ ] Is authority legitimacy assessed?
- [ ] Is communication clarity assessed?
- [ ] Are trust and uncertainty assessed?
- [ ] Are competing standards documented?
- [ ] Are timing and sequencing requirements documented?
- [ ] Are adaptation burdens assessed?
- [ ] Are marginalized communities visible in the analysis?
- [ ] Are qualitative sources included?
- [ ] Are historical sources included?
- [ ] Are assumptions documented?
- [ ] Is uncertainty documented?
- [ ] Are results framed as interpretive rather than deterministic?
- [ ] Is the analysis free from workplace surveillance or individual assessment uses?
