# Adaptation Burden Note

Coordination often distributes switching costs unequally.

A coordination-burden analysis should ask:

- Who must adapt to the selected equilibrium?
- Who selected the focal point?
- Who benefits from standardization?
- Who pays switching, training, or compliance costs?
- Whose local knowledge is displaced?
- Whose dissent is treated as misalignment?
- Who can challenge or revise the standard?
- Are low-resource actors given transition support?

A coordinated system can be efficient while imposing unjust adaptation burdens.
