# Distributional Burden Note

Public goods systems can be collectively beneficial while still distributing burdens unjustly.

A distributional burden analysis should ask:

- Who pays?
- Who benefits?
- Who is monitored?
- Who is punished?
- Who performs hidden labor?
- Whose contribution is voluntary?
- Whose contribution is coerced?
- Whose needs define the public good?
- Which communities experience the system as extraction rather than cooperation?

A public good for some can be a burden-shifting system for others.
