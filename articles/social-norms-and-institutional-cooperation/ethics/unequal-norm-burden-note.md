# Unequal Norm Burden Note

Social norms distribute burdens.

A norm-burden analysis should ask:

- Who must adapt to the norm?
- Who defines appropriate behavior?
- Who is sanctioned most severely?
- Who receives the benefit of the doubt?
- Whose dissent is treated as deviance?
- Who must perform emotional, cultural, or reputational labor?
- Is the norm supporting cooperation or suppressing accountability?
- Can marginalized actors safely challenge the norm?

A norm can be strong, widely shared, and still unjust.
