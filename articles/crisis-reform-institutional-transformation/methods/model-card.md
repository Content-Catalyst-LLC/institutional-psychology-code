# Model Card: Crisis Reform Transformation Scaffold

## Model purpose

This scaffold demonstrates how synthetic institutional crisis data can be used to explore reform probability and transformation depth.

## Intended users

- researchers
- students
- policy analysts
- public-interest technologists
- institutional scholars
- governance researchers

## Out-of-scope users

- employers evaluating workers
- agencies ranking people or communities
- punitive compliance teams
- automated decision systems
- surveillance systems

## Inputs

Synthetic values representing institutional crisis and reform conditions.

## Outputs

- transformation score
- reform probability
- major reform indicator
- deep transformation indicator
- capture warning cases
- distributional attention diagnostics

## Limitations

- synthetic data only
- no real causal validation
- no automatic policy recommendation
- no moral or legal responsibility assignment
- no substitute for historical or qualitative research

## Ethical risks

- legitimacy laundering
- false precision
- ignoring marginalized communities
- over-reliance on administrative indicators
- using reform models to justify preselected conclusions
