# Transition Burden Note

Institutional change can impose uneven burdens.

A transition-burden analysis should ask:

- Who is asked to change behavior?
- Who receives support during transition?
- Who experiences confusion, overload, or loss of access?
- Who benefits from change?
- Who bears hidden labor?
- Whose feedback is ignored?
- Does adaptation reduce harm or shift it elsewhere?
- Are marginalized communities included in defining success?

A system can adapt while becoming less fair.
