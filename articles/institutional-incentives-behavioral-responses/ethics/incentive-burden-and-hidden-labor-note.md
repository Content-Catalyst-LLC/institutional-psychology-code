# Incentive Burden and Hidden Labor Note

Incentive systems distribute reward, burden, visibility, status, and risk.

An incentive-burden analysis should ask:

- Who defines success?
- Who benefits from the incentive?
- Who carries workload burden?
- Who carries documentation burden?
- Who absorbs risk when the incentive fails?
- Whose labor is invisible?
- Who can challenge the metric?
- Who is pressured to optimize a proxy?
- Does the incentive reward public value or private advantage?
- Does the system distinguish capacity constraints from low motivation?

An incentive system can be formally neutral and substantively unequal.
