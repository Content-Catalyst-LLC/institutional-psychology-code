# Burden Inequality Note

Collective action systems distribute burdens.

A burden analysis should ask:

- Who contributes?
- Who benefits?
- Who is monitored?
- Who is blamed when cooperation fails?
- Who performs hidden labor?
- Who carries risk?
- Who has capacity to comply?
- Who can challenge the cooperation rules?
- Are marginalized actors treated as partners or implementation targets?

A collective action system can be successful in aggregate and still be unjust.
