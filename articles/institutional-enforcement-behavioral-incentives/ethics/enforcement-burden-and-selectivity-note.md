# Enforcement Burden and Selectivity Note

Enforcement systems distribute scrutiny, burden, and consequence.

An enforcement-burden analysis should ask:

- Who must understand the rule?
- Who must document compliance?
- Who pays the cost of compliance?
- Who is monitored most closely?
- Who can negotiate flexibility?
- Who can absorb penalties?
- Who can appeal decisions?
- Who is protected when enforcement works?
- Who is exposed when enforcement fails?
- Does enforcement reach powerful actors or only visible actors?

An enforcement system can be formally equal and substantively unequal.
