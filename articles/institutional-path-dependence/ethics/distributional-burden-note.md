# Distributional Burden Note

Path dependence can preserve unequal harm under the language of continuity.

A path-dependence analysis should ask:

- Who benefits from the inherited path?
- Who bears the cost of continuity?
- Whose burden is described as normal?
- Whose knowledge is ignored?
- Who has authority to define reform as realistic?
- Which communities experience the path as constraint rather than stability?
- Does reform reduce lock-in or shift its burdens elsewhere?

A system can be historically stable and morally indefensible at the same time.
